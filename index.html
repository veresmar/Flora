about us

FLORA-RA is the family company founded in 2014.
Our company is a direct supplier of roses from Kenya, from the sunny plains of the Rift Valley. When we bring you flowers in large quantities, we manage to keep our prices as low as possible.
Our team consists of professionals: farmers who grow flowers of the Dutch variety Rhodes for over 10 years, and florists who carefully pack you flowers.
